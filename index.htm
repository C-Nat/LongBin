Long Bin GI progress

The Vision

To have a comprehensive, fairly accurate map that not only consolidates all the useful information including:

  • Borehole locations
  • Pile layout
  • Aerial photos
  • Drilling status(completed, ongoing, pending)
  • Handover area/programme
  • or any other helpful stuff we want


    • but also easily interpretable and accesible on site or anywhere using phone/tablet/laptop.




    Why?

  • Difficult to pinpoint the exact drill hole from 200+ drill holes during discussion with informations scattered in different drawings
  • To identify suitable drilling location which is difficult to be spotted on site with all the ongoing construction works
  • To avoid inefficient time used and efforts in vain trying to mark drillholes on site using only paper drawings/plan
  • To have a sense of bearing and be able to access all the useful information while on field(site)
  • To be able to make immediate ad hoc relocations with reference to the pile layout
  • To have time to immerse in cool rocks and minerals(wanna find gold and be sipping mai tais in Hawaiiüçπ)
